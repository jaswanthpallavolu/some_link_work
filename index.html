Spectrum War May – June Edition

IEEE linkis back with SPECTRUM WAR 2021, May-June edition.

Spectrum war is a quiz contest conducted by the IEEE LINK. The quiz will be based on the topics described in the latest May and June issues of the Spectrum Magazine. The objective of Spectrum War is to assess your grip of the technical concepts introduced in the current issues of the Spectrum Magazine.

NOTE: You can attempt the quiz from 6:00 pm on 30 June 2021 to 11:59 pm on 5 July 2021.

Rules

  1. There will be a total of 30 questions
  2. Once you start the quiz, there will be 30 minutes to complete the quiz
  3. Each question is timed and you will get 1 minute for each question to attempt
